About Me

F.A.Q.

    Where are you based?
  • My business and I currently reside in Las Vegas, Nevada.
    • What are your pieces made from?
  • A majority of my jewelry is creating using Swarovski crystal or handmade glass beads. I wire my jewelry by hand following my clients wishes.
    • When did you begin creating jewelry?
  • I have always been interested in the making of jewelry, and decided to try it for myself around age sixteen. It started off with just basic beads and ribbons, and now I find myself most talented with wire.
    • Why do you create?
  • Growing up, I found it hard to find the jewelry I envisioned in my head. I was very creative and knew exactly what I wanted, but it was always impossible to find what I looked for. By designing my jewelry myself, I always ended up with what I needed.This is why I firmly believe in working with my clients to create unique pieces for any occasion.
    • Do you make pre-made pieces?
  • Yes, all of my current pieces are under the 'Designs' tab.
    • How do you price?
  • Depending on the type and size of crystals you are using, my pieces can range from $5-$35 dollars.
